Mission

The pace of technological change is accelerating. How will New Hampshire adapt and thrive in this rapidly evolving landscape?

The Emerging Technologies Caucus is dedicated to fostering a bipartisan, bicameral commitment among members of the State Legislature to establish New Hampshire as a leading jurisdiction for emerging technologies. The Caucus focuses on educating policymakers and regulators about the innovation landscape, supporting workforce development, and safeguarding consumer privacy through robust protections.

Our Caucus embraces a "techno-optimist" approach, championing progress and innovation while ensuring thoughtful governance to maximize societal and economic benefits. By promoting collaboration and forward-thinking policymaking, the Caucus seeks to position New Hampshire as a national leader in technology-driven economic growth and sustainable innovation.

Our Initiatives

  • Advancing the adoption of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
  • Promoting blockchain technology and its many applications.
  • Supporting the development of small modular reactors (SMRs) and clean energy technologies.
  • Encouraging government efficiency through innovation and technology adoption.
  • Protecting consumer data privacy and promoting cybersecurity best practices.
  • Supporting STEM education and workforce development initiatives.
  • Advocating for policies that promote technology entrepreneurship and economic growth.
