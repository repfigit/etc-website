About Us

The New Hampshire Emerging Technology Caucus is a bipartisan group dedicated to understanding and fostering policies that promote innovation in emerging technologies. Our mission is to educate lawmakers, support innovative businesses, and ensure the responsible integration of new technologies into society.

Our Initiatives

  • Promoting blockchain technology and its applications.
  • Advancing the adoption of artificial intelligence and machine learning.
  • Supporting the development of small modular reactors (SMRs) and clean energy technologies.
  • Encouraging cybersecurity best practices across industries.
