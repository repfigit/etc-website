Mission

The Emerging Technologies Caucus is dedicated to fostering a bipartisan, bicameral commitment to establishing New Hampshire as a leading jurisdiction for emerging technologies. The Caucus focuses on educating policymakers and regulators about the rapidly evolving innovation landscape, supporting workforce development, and safeguarding consumer privacy through robust protections.

In addition, the Caucus emphasizes a “techno-optimist” approach, embracing technological progress and innovation while ensuring thoughtful governance to maximize societal and economic benefits. By promoting collaboration and forward-thinking policymaking, the Caucus aims to position New Hampshire as a national leader in technology-driven economic growth and responsible innovation.
