Mission

The pace of technological change is accelerating. How will New Hampshire adapt and thrive in this rapidly evolving landscape?

The Emerging Technologies Caucus is dedicated to fostering a bipartisan, bicameral commitment among members of the State Legislature to establish New Hampshire as a leading jurisdiction for emerging technologies. The Caucus focuses on educating policymakers and regulators about the innovation landscape, supporting workforce development, and safeguarding consumer privacy through robust protections.

Our Caucus embraces a Techno-Optimism approach, championing progress and innovation while ensuring thoughtful governance to maximize societal and economic benefits. By promoting collaboration and forward-thinking policymaking, the Caucus seeks to position New Hampshire as a national leader in technology-driven economic growth and sustainable innovation.

Initiatives:

  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Blockchain technology and its many applications
  • Advanced nuclear and clean energy technologies
  • Government efficiency through innovation and technology adoption
  • Consumer data privacy and cybersecurity best practices
  • STEM education and workforce development
  • Technology entrepreneurship and economic growth
